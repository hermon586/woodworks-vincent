About Us

Woodworks by Vincent is a small woodworking shop in West Des Moines, Iowa. We create custom, handcrafted pieces ranging from cutting boards and wine racks to furniture and home décor. Our mission is to combine functionality with artistry, using locally sourced materials whenever possible and finishing each piece to last a lifetime.

Decorative Pieces

Our Process

From timber selection to the final finish, each piece goes through an artisanal journey.

Sourcing & Design

We choose locally sourced hardwoods and collaborate with you to design pieces that suit your style and space.

Handcraft & Assembly

Each board is carefully cut, shaped and assembled by hand using traditional joinery techniques for lasting strength.

Finishing Touches

We sand and finish every surface with food‑safe oils and waxes to enhance the natural beauty of the wood.
